FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on complexity. A landing page takes 3-5 days, a custom website 1-3 weeks, a SaaS product 4-8 weeks, and an AI-powered application 3-6 weeks. I'll give you an accurate timeline during our discovery call.

What technologies do you specialize in?

My core stack includes React, Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript, and AI APIs (OpenAI, LangChain). For databases I use MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and Firebase. I deploy on Vercel, AWS, and Netlify.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! Every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. I also offer ongoing maintenance packages for long-term support.

How do you handle payments?

I work with a milestone-based payment structure. Typically 50% upfront to begin work, and 50% on completion. For larger projects, we can break payments into 3-4 milestones.

Can you work with my existing team?

Absolutely. I integrate seamlessly with existing development teams, designers, and project managers. I'm comfortable with Git workflows, code reviews, and agile sprints.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, I'm happy to sign NDAs and confidentiality agreements. Your ideas and data are always safe with me.
